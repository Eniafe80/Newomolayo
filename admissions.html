Enroll Your Child at Omolayo International Schools

Thank you for considering Omolayo International Schools for your child’s education. We welcome children from diverse backgrounds into a nurturing and values-based learning environment.

Admission Process

  1. Inquiry & Tour – Visit the school or contact us to speak with an administrator.
  2. Application Form – Complete and submit the admission form (online or offline).

    3. Online Admission Form

  3. Entrance Assessment – The student will take an entrance test appropriate to their grade level.
  4. Interview – A brief interaction with the student and parents.
  5. Admission Offer – Upon successful evaluation, admission is offered.
  6. Enrollment – Pay school fees and receive uniform and school starter pack.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • 2 Passport Photographs
  • Previous Academic Records (if applicable)
  • Completed Admission Form
