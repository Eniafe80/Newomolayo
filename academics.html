Our Academic Excellence

At Omolayo International Schools, we take pride in offering a balanced curriculum that combines both Nigerian and international best practices. Our academic structure is designed to support a child’s growth intellectually, emotionally, and morally.

Programmes Offered

  • Nursery & Early Years: Foundational skills through play-based and Montessori methods
  • Primary School: Core subjects including Literacy, Numeracy, Science, Social Studies, ICT, and Moral Education
  • Junior Secondary: A broad-based curriculum preparing students for external examinations and leadership roles

Academic Features

  • Small class sizes for personal attention
  • STEAM integration (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics)
  • Language enrichment (English, Yoruba, and French)
  • Regular assessment and progress tracking
  • Homework support and after-school coaching

We are proud of our students’ performances in national and international assessments.
